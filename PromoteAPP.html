Main Interface Display

In this fast-paced world, sleep quality is often neglected. We bring you a new, natural way to help you sleep - DIY fragrance. Through personalized fragrance formulas, accurate sleep data tracking, and community interaction, we help you improve your sleep quality every night.








Personalized Fragrance Page


You can customize your own fragrance based on the scent you like or need on this interface. After customization, you can choose to try smell or save.



Sleep Tracking Calendar Page


This interface can help you record your daily sleep status. It also includes daily, weekly, monthly and annual calendars, and can also record what kind of fragrance you use every day.



Forum Page


You can share your own fragrances or view fragrances recommended by others on this interface. You can also view the proportions or recommendations of fragrances blended by others in detail, and can save or share with friends.



Contact Page


The last page is the friend interface, where you can add friends and communicate with them to share good fragrances.
